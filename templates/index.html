Understanding Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect your heart. It remains a critical global health issue, but one that is often manageable and preventable.

  • Global Impact: Heart disease is the leading cause of death for men and women worldwide.
  • Key Types: It includes conditions like Coronary Artery Disease (CAD), heart failure, and arrhythmias (irregular heartbeat).
  • Risk Factors: Key risks include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, diabetes, obesity, and family history.
  • Prevention is Key: Many forms of heart disease can be prevented or managed with healthy lifestyle choices.
  • Our Goal: This tool assesses your risk based on key clinical factors, empowering you to start an informed conversation with a healthcare professional.
